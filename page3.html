Sharpie Fine Point Marker
Constraints
What can’t you do with a Sharpie?

Well, for starters, you can’t see how much ink you’ve got left. It’s always a mystery!

The ink is also not easily washable – it is waterproof for up to a week on skin, and it can stain materials such as fabrics forever. Be careful!

Its functionality is also heavily reliant on user responsibility. If a user leaves this marker uncapped for too long, they may dry it out!

Finally, not everyone can handle this kind of marker. If you passed these out in a kindergarten class, the kids might cause a respectable amount of property damage.
